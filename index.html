How The Heck Do I Become A Space Captain

A space adventure where you must become the captain of your own space base, learn to lead your troops against the enemy in life or death battles in adverse environments while you improve and control your entire space team. Try it yourself!!

How The Heck Do I Become A Space Captain is a strategy turn base combat in a hexagon board
with a retrofuturistic esthetic and enjoyable characters made for everyone to love.
