How The Heck Do I Become A Space Captain

A space adventure where you'll become the captain of your own spaceship, learn to lead your troops against the enemy in life or death battles in adverse environments while you improve and control your entire space team.
Try it yourself!!

How The Heck Do I Become A Space Captain is a strategy turn-based combat game set on a hexagon grid board
with a retrofuturistic aesthetic and enjoyable characters made for everyone to love.

Meet the crew

The mushroom alien
As grumpy as he may seem, he has a soft side.
Help your crew members giving them an extra turn with his powerful spores!

The space explorer
Jump into a space adventure with this lovely explorer and her cute sidekick
Give Calamari to your allies in combat to heal them at the start of each turn

A sentient space suit!?
This cryptid creature only wants to be loved, so give them a lot of afection
In the battleground the'll act as a sturdy wall that protects their allies, being able to increase their defense

The helpful robot
G470 is always there to help the ones in danger
She focuses on healing and helping allies, so always keep an eye on her and don't let her die in combat

The guy
This rascal is on everyone's lips, mostly for bad reasons
Norbert lives on the edge of the moment, he has a powerful attack that may or may not work, just hope you get lucky!

The brainless fish girl
Not a single thought in her head, she explodes everything that stands in her way
Expert in long distance combat, she sets the enemies on fire with a forceful shoot from her rocket launcher


The differents levels

The arboreal planet

Play in this planet full of life with exotic creatures and plants
In this level your objetive will be to end with all the enemies without your allies being killed

The enemies

Scary and predatory, this alien has the ability to get through all the obstacles thanks to their superior agility

The invasion of the Keus deep

Keus is a rich mineral city and the next victim of a famous group of invaders
The objetive of this level is to defend a city from this invaders in a limited time while the force camp of the city is being formed

The enemies

These invasive aliens are from another galaxy and travel around the universe to devour life from other planets. They always come in groups

The infested spaceship

This level takes place in a Federation spaceship infested with parasites that try to eat everything
The objetive of this level is to eliminate all enemies before they escape to infect other spaceships

The enemies

This type of parasite is actually harmless to people, but not to the infrastructure of the ships!

The final boss

A mysterious life form named HARNCKXSHOR claimed as his home an antique and culture rich ruin and doesn’t let explorers enter it.
In this level your objetive will be to defeat HARNCKXSHOR

The enemies

HARNCKXSHOR its an unknown life form, his body is a sticky mass that can be turned to any desired form and can be separated to form small non-intellegent creatures
